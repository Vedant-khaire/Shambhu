2. Shipping and Delivery

We process all retail orders within 1-3 business days. Delivery times vary based on your location within India but typically range from 3-7 business days. Wholesale and custom printed orders may require additional processing time.

3. Returns and Refunds

We accept returns for defective or damaged products if reported within 7 days of delivery with an unboxing video. Refunds will be processed to the original source of payment within 5-7 working days of receiving the returned goods.
