Our Story

Crafting Quality for Decades

Established in Pune, Maharashtra, Shambhu Files has been a trusted name in manufacturing premium office filing solutions. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to organize India's offices, courts, and schools with durable, high-quality, and affordable products.

We pride ourselves on using top-grade raw materials, ensuring that every Box File, Ring Binder, and Spring File we produce meets stringent quality standards. From humble beginnings, our manufacturing unit has expanded significantly, and today we are proud to supply our products Pan-India.
